Haikyū!

Former powerhouse highschool volleyball Team Karasuno makes a comeback and flies to the Spring Tournament

Information

Haikyū! is an anime series based on the manga that was created and drawn by Furudate Haruichi. The story starts off with Hinata Shōyō, a junior high school boy who watches Karasuno's "Little Giant" in a volleyball match on television. He makes it his goal to become the next "Little Giant", joining his junior high volleyball club and then the Karasuno volleyball team in highschool.
